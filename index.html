Top 10 Budget-Friendly Hotels in Colombo for 2025 Travelers

1. ME Colombo

A trendy and modern hotel with a romantic ambiance, highly rated for its excellent service and stylish design.

2. Mirage Hotel Colombo

Offering stunning ocean and bay views, this mid-range hotel is a favorite for travelers seeking comfort and scenic surroundings.

3. Grand Oriental Hotel

A classic hotel with a rich history, providing straightforward accommodations in a convenient location.

4. Ivy Lane Colombo

This hotel is ideal for business travelers and is centrally located and praised for its quiet, professional atmosphere.

5. CityRest Fort

A budget-friendly option in the heart of the city, known for its simplicity and peaceful environment.

6. Pearl Grand By Rathna

A smaller hotel with a welcoming vibe, appreciated for its attentive service and cozy feel.

7. Fairview Hotel

Combining modern amenities with a relaxed setting, this hotel is perfect for those seeking a quiet, mid-range stay.

8. Trillium Boutique City Hotel

A charming boutique hotel with a trendy and romantic appeal, offering a unique and intimate experience.

9. Best Western Elyon Colombo

Located in a residential neighborhood, this hotel provides a quiet retreat with contemporary comforts.

10. Miracle City Inn

A no-frills, budget-friendly hotel with a quaint and simple charm, ideal for cost-conscious travelers.
